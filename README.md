A minimalistic, lightweight, offline-first password manager designed to keep your credentials secure, private, and entirely under your control.

## 🌱 Motivation

Ajikhoji's Password Manager was originally created to solve a personal problem: managing hundreds of credentials stored across spreadsheets.

What began as a solution to a personal need gradually evolved into a fully encrypted, offline-first password manager focused on **simplicity, privacy, and security**.

## ✨ Features

* 🔐 **Secure encrypted storage** for account credentials
* 📝 **Notes and custom fields** for storing additional account details
* ⚡ **Fast filtering and sorting** for quickly finding credentials
* 🎨 **Customizable views** for a more personalized experience
* 📥 **Import and export** account credentials
* 🏷️ **Labels** for organizing and categorizing accounts
* 📊 **Dashboard insights** for a quick overview of your stored credentials

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

Your data should belong to you.

Unlike cloud-based password managers, this application keeps your encrypted vault on your own device. No account creation, internet connection, or remote server is required to use the application.

* 🚫 No cloud storage
* 🚫 No advertisements
* 🚫 No tracking
* 🚫 No telemetry
* 🔐 Encryption and decryption happen locally on your device

Your credentials remain under your control.
